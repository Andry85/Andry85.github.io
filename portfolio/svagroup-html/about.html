About us

On the 01st and 02nd of March 2013 the annual meeting of the Sintec company was held.

The first day was devoted to the analysis of the results of the Sintec Company in 2012 and the setting of key strategic decisions for the further development of the company. Subsidiary of Sintec in Great Britain brought to the participants the results of operations abroad. The participants of the first day were top managers and executive management of Sintec UK.

The second day was held with the participation of middle management. Top Management of the company announced key performance indicators for 2012 and the main decisions taken on the first day of the meeting. Many participants came up with initiatives aimed at improving the efficiency of units of the Sintec Company. According to the results in 2012 the best employees were granted and qualified as HQP (high – qualified personnel).

It is important to note the steady growth and consolidation of the company’s position in the market of wide range of integrated services in electrical and mechanical engineering, design and installation of production equipment for projects of any complexity over the territory of Russian Federation and abroad. 2012 year was significant for the reaching of milliard of working capital. Another trend is that good results of contracts at Russian Federation territory are spreading also for abroad offices.
