About us

Sintec LLC is a Russian company that provides a wide range of integrated services in electrical and mechanical engineering, design and installation of production equipment for projects of any complexity over the territory of Russian Federation, Europe, United Kingdom and the United States of America. Since 1997 when Sintec was founded, the company focuses on procurement and installation of various kinds of power supply systems, any type of communications, security and automation systems for industrial and commercial facilities.

Sintec offers to its Customers innovative solution in a widest selection of engineering systems ranging between: engineering and installation of computer networks and security systems, design, installation and commissioning of power distribution networks, high-voltage and low-voltage lines, internal and external transformer substations. SINTEC uses materials and equipment manufactured in Europe and Russia whilst the package of licenses in the portfolio allows SINTEC to carry out our activities all over the Russian Federation and Europe.

Sintec Customers are known world wide: German, French, Austrian, Finnish, Belgian and British construction and manufacturing companies that operate on Russian and European production markets.
