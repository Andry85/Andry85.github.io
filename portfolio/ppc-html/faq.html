FAQ

What is Dogma PPC?

UmaxSearch is a Pay Per Click search engine where every search result carries a bid value. Results are sorted descending by bid values that are set by advertisers.

How does UmaxSearch affiliate program work?

Traffic partners (webmasters) can send adult or non-adult traffic to UmaxSearch search engine and get 70% of the clicks that referred surfers initiate. UmaxSearch has a webmaster referral program which is recurring 5% of what referred webmasters make.

How do I get paid?

We issue bi-weekly payments with bank-wires, international money orders or WebMoney.

Why do I see no/less results on popular keywords?

We have different bids for different countries, and countries with lower spending habbits have less or none bids. Highest bids are for US, Canadian, and Western-European traffic.

What if I do not know how to install it or need more features?

We will help you implement the system and fit it exactly for your website(s) if needed.

Why UmaxSearch and not competition?

UmaxSearch uses 12 search feeds from different bid sources. We merge their feeds with our own bids and generate results. We always pay on time, even though we get paid about 2 months late. We give fair revenue sharing percentages. If you are still not convinced - contact us.
