Who we are

AlphaSales is a small company located here in the heart of Ramat-Gan. Surrounded by start-ups, we seized upon an opportunity to work right where finance and hi-tech converge. We specialize in providing sales and marketing services operations within the financial sector.

At AlphaSales, we know that our product is only as good as our employees so we make sure to carefully select our most important assets- you. The founders of AlphaSales had worked in companies where employees were viewed as expendable and wanted to make a change with their new company. At AlphaSales we have made it a priority to ensure our sales and marketing force are welcomed, supported, and encouraged to grow.

Do you think you have what it takes to join our companies? AlphaSales is looking for creative, motivated problem-solvers who are ready to take on unique challenges. If this sounds like you and you are ready to join a dynamic team environment, then contact us for more information.
