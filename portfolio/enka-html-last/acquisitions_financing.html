Silverstein Properties, Inc. (SPI) has been involved in acquisitions encompassing 20+ million square feet of real estate including the $3.2 billion bid on the WTC in 2001, the largest deal in New York's history at the time.

With reliable and consistent sources of capital from high-net worth individuals, pension funds, international banks, sovereign wealth funds, hedge funds, insurance companies, and private equity funds, SPI has secured billions of dollars in debt and equity to finance existing properties and development projects. In addition, the firm has expertise using a variety of economic development programs supported by federal, state and city agencies in the New York Metropolitan region.

SPI is open to new acquisition and joint venture opportunities. Please send a brief description of your offering and contact information to ndangelo@silvprop.com.
