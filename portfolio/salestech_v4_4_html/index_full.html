Who are we?

Banc De Binary is a leading global investment house that specializes in binary option trading. We were founded in 2009, and last year became the world’s first regulated binary options firm. Today, we work with customers and business partners in over 100 countries worldwide. Our office in the Diamond District in Ramat Gan enjoys close collaboration with our sister offices in Cyprus and London.

We are very proud to be constantly innovating and leading this industry forwards. And we want to remain at the top. That’s why we only recruit the best – from our highly driven sales and marketing staff to our outstanding programmers and web designers. We value those who share our vision and standards, and who want to play an active role in building the company’s future in the financial industry.
