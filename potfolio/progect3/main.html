Who We Are.

RexMedia Ltd is an internationally recognized company that works to merge pioneering product development, inventive applications, and the fore-most minds in online marketing together in order to create the highest quality product for our clientele. We serve our clients in everything from creating products, advertising campaigns for the global financial markets, and call centers that focus on helping investors understanding financial markets.

Our marketing team is consistently creating visionary campaigns that help to bring our products into the forefront of the market. Their dynamic and wide range of skills has helped to inspire campaigns that are targeted for mobile and desktop. This means that our client base is provided with the absolute highest quality service in the business. RexMedia Ltd. takes only the best employees from the fields of development, creative designers, and sales/customer service.

We are conveniently located in Tel Aviv and are always ready to hear from bright and creative thinkers who can bring fresh ideas to our company and grow with us. If you are looking for a dynamic work environment and believe you would be a good fit for our company, send us your CV.
