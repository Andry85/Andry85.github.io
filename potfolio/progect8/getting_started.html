Step 2 Deposit Funds

The next step towards successful trading is depositing funds into
your account.
We offer many easy and cost effective deposit methods, and you
can choose the currency you want your account to work with.

DEPOSIT FUNDS

Step 3 Start Trading

After choosing your prefered LiveTrader™ platform, your trading experience can begin.

  • Choose an asset.

    You can choose from a variety of assets by clicking on the asset dropdown menu.

  • Expiry time selection.

    Please select the time and date at which an option expires.

  • Investment Amount.

    Enter the amount you wish to invest – anywhere from $12 up to $20,000

  • Live graphics.

    You can follow live on the instantaneous value of the option in question.

  • Potential Return.

    Potential payout and percentage return of the option.

  • Select the direction of investment.

    Choose between buying a "call" or a "put" option on any asset which you want to trade.

GO TO TRADE PAGE

Step 4 Wait for Expiry

You can follow your position all the way to expiry with the help of the graph at Today’s
Open Positions that is located on the bottom part of the page.

You will know at every moment whether you are on or off target. After expiry check for results
in My Portfolio at the top right corner of the page.

An email with your trade’s result will be automatically sent to your email address.
